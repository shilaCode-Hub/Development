About Ferrari

Ferrari is one of the most iconic and prestigious car manufacturers in the world, 
known for its luxury sports cars and rich racing heritage. Founded in 1947 by Enzo Ferrari in Maranello, 
Italy, the brand quickly became synonymous with speed, elegance, and cutting-edge engineering. 
Ferrari's signature red color, called "Rosso Corsa," and its prancing horse logo are recognized globally.
Ferrari has a long and successful history in Formula One racing, holding numerous championships and records.
Its cars, such as the Ferrari 488, LaFerrari, and SF90 Stradale, are admired for their design, performance, and exclusivity. 
Each model combines advanced technology with Italian craftsmanship, making Ferrari a dream car for many automobile enthusiasts.
